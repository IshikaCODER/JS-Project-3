Guessing Game


Step 1: Click on the Start button

Step 2: Enter the maximum number

Step 3: Enter your guess

Step 4: Enter "quit" if you want to quit the game




{Description: In this game, the player has to enter the maximum number and then has to guess the number generated by the computer/ system. The hint will also be provided according to the answer given by the player. If you want to quit, you can quit the game by simply typing "quit".}
